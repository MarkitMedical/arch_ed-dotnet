Languages and terminologies screen

The languages and terminologies screen provides information about available languages and terminologies.

Languages

You can:
  • View what is the primary language of the archetype
  • View and add the available languages - press the button to add the language
  • Choose to view the archetype in a particular language by clicking on the language in the language list


Terminology

You can:
  • View available terminologies
  • Add a terminology
  • View the terms bound to a particular terminology by clicking on the [+] symbol to the left of the terminology in the grid.

  • This will display the list of relationships - term and constraint bindings

  • Click on these to see terms bound to this terminology

  • Click on the small white left pointing arrow on the right of the Available terminologies panel (shown in the top left of this image) to return to a list of terminologies.
