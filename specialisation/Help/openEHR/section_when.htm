When to use a SECTION

A SECTION is an organising class, contained within a COMPOSITION. Archetypes of Sections standardise the organisation of information within a Composition.

Examples of Sections are:

  • SOAP(E) organisation of the EHR
  • Physical examination - organised by System
  • History - organised by presenting complaint, social history, review of systems etc

In summary, if you are attempting to standardise the organisation of information within a document, progress note or any other Composition, then you probably need to create an SECTION archetype.
