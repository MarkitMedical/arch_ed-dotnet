Add a language

To add a language to an archetype either:

  • Choose Menu > Language > Add, or
  • Move to the 'Terminology Tab', select the 'Languages and Terminologies' Tab beneath that and press the button on the left by 'Available languages'

A screen with a list of languages will appear.

Move the cursor to the language required and press OK. The language may be a sub-type of a more universal language (as is shown by Arabic in the screen above).

You will be asked to confirm the addition of a new language unless the language is already present.
