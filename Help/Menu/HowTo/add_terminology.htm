Add a terminology

To add a terminology to an archetype either:

  • Choose Menu > Terminology > Add, or
  • Move to the 'Terminology Tab', select the 'Languages and Terminologies' Tab beneath that and press the button on the right by 'Available terminologies'.

A screen with a list of terminologies as described by the National Library of Medicine in the USA will appear.

Move the cursor to the terminology required and press OK. 

You will be asked to confirm the addition of a new terminology unless the terminology is already present.
