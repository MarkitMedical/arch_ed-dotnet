Interval event

Events such as maximum's and minimum's cover a period of time with a width and are called interval measurements. To set an event to an interval measurement choose the Interval button on the right and then choose the type of interval measurement. Examples of interval measurements might be a maximum reading over a 24 hour period such as pulse rate, a total reading over an 24 hour period such as blood loss or fluid intake, or a change in weight such as wieght loss over a month.
